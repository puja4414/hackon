Our Recycling Process

See how we transform plastic waste into valuable materials

1

Collection & Sorting

We collect plastic waste from homes, businesses, and collection centers, then sort it by type and color.

2

Cleaning & Shredding

Plastic is thoroughly cleaned to remove contaminants, then shredded into small flakes.

3

Melting & Extrusion

The flakes are melted and extruded into pellets or directly formed into new products.

4

Manufacturing

The recycled material is used to create high-quality construction materials and consumer products.
